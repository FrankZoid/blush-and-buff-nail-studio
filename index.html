Welcome to Blush & Buff

Clean, Comfortable & Approachable

Blush & Buff Nail Studio is a friendly neighborhood nail salon in Richmond, Virginia, offering manicures, pedicures, gel polish, dip powder, acrylics, builder gel, and custom nail art. Whether you are booking a quick polish refresh or a full set with detailed art, our goal is to make your visit comfortable, clean, and easy.

Blush & Buff Nail Studio is a friendly neighborhood nail salon focused on clean, comfortable services and polished results. From everyday manicures and relaxing pedicures to gel, dip powder, acrylics, builder gel, and custom nail art, our team helps clients feel put together for work, weekends, special occasions, and everything in between.

Clean, Comfortable Nail Care

Clean tools, tidy stations, and client comfort are part of every appointment. Tools are cleaned and sanitized between services, disposable items are used where appropriate, and manicure and pedicure areas are reset throughout the day to keep the salon fresh, organized, and welcoming.
